STUDENT PROFILE

Allison Rodrigues, Student

Allison Rodrigues is a second year Interaction Design and Development student at George Brown College. Most recently, she was a Program Coordinator at the not-for-profit cultural institution Design Exchange. She created and delivered daily-multidisciplinary design workshops and activities for hundreds of kids over the summer. Her professional experience includes teaching design and technology to youth in low income areas around Toronto. On her free time, Allison bakes different kinds of breads using her own starter out of her little kitchen in East York.

PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

Interaction Design is an interdisciplinary field merging design skills such as user experience, interface development and graphic design with coding fundamentals to create meaningful experiences between the user and digital platforms such as social media, websites, wearables and mobile devices.
