Student Profile

Allison Rodrigues

Allison Rodrigues is a second year Interaction Design and Development student at George Brown College. Her studies focus on user interface and user experience design. Most recently, she was a Program Coordinator at the not-for-profit cultural institution Design Exchange. She created and delivered daily-multidisciplinary design workshops and activities for hundreds of kids over the summer. Her professional experience includes teaching design and technology to youth in low income areas around Toronto. Currently, she focuses on her studies on user interface and user experience design at George Brown College. On her free time, Allison bakes different kinds of breads using her own starter out of her little kitchen in East York.

Course Description

Interaction Design is an interdisciplinary field merging design skills such as user experience, interface development and graphic design with coding fundamentals to create meaningful experiences between the user and digital platforms such as social media, websites, wearables and mobile devices.

Career Oppurtunities

Graduates of this program will have acquired the skills necessary to be employed in various digital media studios, with careers such as:

  • Information Architect
  • Interface Designer
  • Mobile Designer
  • Mobile Developer
  • Production Artist
  • 3D Visualization Artist
  • Motion Designer
  • User Experience Designer
  • User Interface Designer
  • Web Designer
  • Web Developer
  • SEO/SEM Specialist
